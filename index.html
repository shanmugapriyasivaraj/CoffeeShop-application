What makes our coffee

we buy our coffee from only the most reputable farms and mills in Kona. Being a teacher in Kona for three decades, I taught some of the farmers and some of their parents too - which goes a long way.

These boutique companies roast all beans of varying size together because they cannot afford to waste any beans regardless of quality and they don’t have enough beans for it to make sense to separate them into different grades.
